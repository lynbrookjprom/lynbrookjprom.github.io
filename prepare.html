Prepare for Prom

Everything you need to be ready for the event

  • Fill out Forms

    Print and fill out the medical and permission formsM. We cannot sell a bid to you unless you have these ready! If you’re bringing a guest, remember to fill out the guest form.

  • Purchase Bids

    Come out on Thursday lunches with your medical and permission forms, a check made payable to Lynbrook ASB, and your ID! :)

  • Gentlemen: Suits

    Along with your bid, you will also be receiving a 15% off coupon for the Tuxedo Wearhouse. They’ll be coming to Lynbrook to fit our models for the fashion show! Go in and get yourself fitted as well! :)

  • Ladies: Dresses

    Shop local or order your dress online and be sure to join the FB JProm Dress Group.

  • Corsages + Boutonnieres

    We've partnered up with our wonderful local florist, La Floriya, to sell corsages for $32 and boutonnieres for $15! Check back here in a few days for a link to order.

  • Professional photography

    ProImage will be taking your professional pictures at the event. The photographer is Mr. Bishop. There will be a form to order pictures released soon, so keep on the lookout for that!

  • Dinner

    Dinner will not be provided at prom! However, we will be ordering a variety of snacks, foods, and general sustenance to meet your hunger needs. Be sure to plan for dinner for either before or after the event.

  • Rides

    You will need to provide your own rides to and from prom. Students are NOT under any circumstances allowed to drive themselves- if you would like to carpool, please arrange your own carpool group to and from the event!
